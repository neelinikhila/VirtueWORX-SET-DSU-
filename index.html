Our Vision

To emerge as a globally recognized center of excellence in engineering education and research, fostering innovation and producing technically competent, socially responsible professionals who can address the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Mission

  • Provide world-class engineering education through innovative teaching methodologies
  • Promote cutting-edge research and development activities
  • Establish strong industry-academia collaborations
  • Develop entrepreneurial skills and ethical values in students
  • Contribute to societal development through technological solutions

Our Journey

2008

Establishment

School of Engineering founded with 3 undergraduate programs in Computer Science, Mechanical, and Electrical Engineering.

2012

First Accreditation

Received NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation, recognizing quality in technical education.

2015

Research Center

Established the Center for Advanced Research with funding from DST and AICTE.

2018

University Status

Upgraded to Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University, expanding engineering programs.

2023

Global Recognition

Ranked among top 100 engineering institutions in India by NIRF.
