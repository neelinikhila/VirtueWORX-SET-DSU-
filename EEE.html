Program Curriculum

The B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering program provides students with a strong foundation in electrical systems, electronics, power engineering, and control systems.

Power Systems

Learn about generation, transmission, and distribution of electrical power.

Electronics

Understand electronic devices, circuits, and embedded systems.

Control Systems

Study automation, robotics, and industrial control systems.
